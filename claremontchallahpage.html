About Claremont Challah

Claremont Challah is a student run organization at Pomona College that makes, bakes, and sells challah bread to the Claremont, CA community twice a week. All challah sale proceeds are donated to food aid organizations in order to help fight food insecurity in Southern California.

Find Claremont Challah on: Facebook    |    Instagram    |    LinkedIn


My Role

I worked as Head Baker as well as the Head of Social Media and Communications from 2018-2022. As Head Baker, I led groups of 4-6 volunteer bakers in making and baking over 150 loaves of challah bread every week.

As Head of Social Media and Communications, I activated the organization's social media accounts and ran the Instagram and Facebook pages, designing and producing multiple posts and promotional material each week to announce and advertise challah sales, events, and increase community engagement. I grew the Instagram account from zero to 1500+ followers, and increased post likes and engagement by over 40% within my first 6 months and by over 100% by my final semester driving social media initiatives.
