About Me


Hi there, thanks for stopping by! ☺

I am a new grad from Pomona College, where I studied computer science and studio art. Most recently, I've worked as a software engineer at Meta. I'm passionate in using my background in software engineering and design to build meaningful user experiences through a human centered design approach. As a software engineer, I have experience in both backend and frontend development, but have always gravitated toward client facing work that combines tech and design.

I enjoy researching and understanding user needs in order to design digital experiences that are not only accesible, efficient, and inclusive but also a delight to use.

When I'm not creating new components on Figma, finding typography inspiration, or coding, I love watching animated movies, baking bread, and crocheting mini hats for my cat.

I make art as well! Check out my art portfolio here.
